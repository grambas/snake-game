Snake Instructions

Introduction

Take control of snake and eat so many viruses as you can. Fight with viruses and do not let them take control! The snake will help you

Rules

  • Increase your snake size by eating viruses
  • Snake can not collide with herself
  • Snake can not collide gameboard boundaries
  • Snake can be turned maximal 90 degrees in one movement

GameOver

The game will end if your snake collides with herself or with gameboard boundaries.

Score

You will earn +1 point for every eaten virus.

Level

There are 3 levels implemented. 1 level is the  slowest  and 3 level is the fastest. Choose Level 2 for balanced speed
